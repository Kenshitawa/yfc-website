About Youth for Children

A youth‑led, community‑based organization advancing child protection, inclusive education, and youth leadership through digital storytelling and grassroots action across Kenya.

Our Story

From a Capstone Project to a Movement

Youth for Children (YFC) was founded in 2023 by Stephen Okoth and Joy Tadayo, who began as teaching assistants through Pacemaker International. While designing a capstone project on educational inequalities, they discovered that many learners were facing different forms of abuse. This led to the creation of the Child Protection and Safeguarding (CPAS) curriculum.

The pilot reached 88 children (40 boys, 46 girls) aged 10–12, but also revealed the widespread need for sustainable action. YFC was born with a unique approach: empowering youth who have overcome systemic challenges to directly protect and empower children.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Approach

Vision

A just and thriving society where every child is protected, educated, and empowered by the leadership of inspired youth.

Mission

Through youth‑led programs, we strengthen child protection, inclusive education, and community development – building a future where young people lead efforts to safeguard and empower the next generation.

Approach

We use digital storytelling, mentorship, and hands‑on community engagement to equip youth with the skills to directly protect children and create safer, inclusive learning environments.

Our Core Values

The Values That Guide Us

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our actions.

Child‑Centeredness

Every decision we make prioritizes the well‑being and best interests of children.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure every child and youth feels valued and heard.

Community Ownership

We empower communities to lead and sustain positive change from within.

Creativity & Innovation

We use digital storytelling and fresh approaches to engage young minds.

Accountability

Collaboration

Empowerment
