Youth for Children Privacy Policy

Clear, practical information about privacy and data use.

Last updated: Feb 2026

1) Information We Collect

We may collect information when you:

  • Make a donation or support our child protection and youth programs
  • Register as a volunteer or apply to join "Empowering the Future"
  • Submit a contact form or request information about our work
  • Subscribe to updates or express interest in our programs

Depending on your interaction, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (email, phone)
  • Optional profile details you submit (e.g., skills, county, interests in child protection or youth leadership)
  • Donation references (e.g., M-Pesa code or bank transfer reference)
  • Basic technical data (IP address, device type, browser) for security and analytics
We do not collect sensitive personal information from children or adults unless it is necessary for a specific program and proper consent is obtained.

2) How We Use Personal Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm donations and keep accurate financial records
  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested information about our programs
  • Send updates or newsletters about our work with children and youth (you can opt out anytime)
  • Improve website performance, security, and user experience
  • Understand general engagement trends (aggregated, not personal profiling)

General browsing of our website is mostly anonymous. We may log basic visit details for diagnostics and security purposes.

3) Sharing & Disclosure

Youth for Children treats your information as confidential. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We may share data only when necessary:

  • With payment providers to complete a transaction
  • With trusted service providers supporting our website operations (hosting, email delivery)
  • If required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and well-being of children and communities we serve

4) Cookie Policy

Cookies are small files stored on your device. They help websites remember your preferences and improve performance.

  • You can accept, reject, or delete cookies through your browser settings
  • Disabling cookies may affect how some site features work

5) Children's Privacy

Protecting children is at the heart of our mission. We take extra care when children interact with our website or programs:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental or guardian consent
  • Photos and stories of children shared on our website or social media are only published with explicit consent from parents or guardians
  • If you believe a child's information has been collected without proper consent, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action
We are committed to upholding the highest standards of child safeguarding in all our data practices, in line with Kenya's Children Act and international child protection standards.

6) Payments & Security

We use recognized payment channels and security practices. Where online payments are used, your data is transmitted securely (e.g., SSL/TLS).

  • We do not store full card details on our servers
  • Transaction references (like M-Pesa codes) may be stored for verification and accounting
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information

7) Third-Party Services

Our website may embed or link to third-party services (e.g., YouTube, Google Maps, social media platforms). These services may collect usage data according to their own policies. Youth for Children is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

8) Your Rights & Data Requests

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete information where applicable
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters or updates

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We aim to respond within a reasonable time.

9) Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
