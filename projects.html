Our Programs

Youth-led initiatives advancing child protection, inclusive education, and environmental stewardship across Kenya.

Home • Programs

Building a Safer, Brighter Future for Every Child

Our programs are designed and led by youth who understand the challenges children face. From child protection to climate action, each initiative puts children's rights and well-being at the centre.

Flagship

Child Protection & Safeguarding

Creating safe environments where children can learn, grow, and thrive free from abuse, neglect, exploitation, and violence. Through awareness sessions, capacity-building workshops, and community engagement, we equip children, caregivers, and educators with the knowledge and skills to recognize, prevent, and respond to child protection concerns.

88 learners reached in pilot 5+ schools

Empowering the Future

Our flagship youth leadership and development program. Through mentorship, public speaking training, storytelling, and practical community engagement, we strengthen young people's voices and equip them with the skills to become confident leaders and advocates for children's well-being.

18 youth leaders trained Public speaking & advocacy

Climate Action (Eco-Heroes)

Equipping children and young people with the knowledge, values, and practical skills to become responsible environmental stewards. Through interactive learning, storytelling, tree-growing, waste management, and conservation activities, we nurture a deep connection between learners and nature.

Tree-growing projects Waste management

One Learner, One Pen

A resource mobilization initiative aimed at supporting children from underserved and informal settlement communities by providing essential learning materials. Through partnerships, donations, and community support, we remove barriers to education by supplying pens, pencils, exercise books, and other school necessities.

Inclusive Education

Creating learning environments where every child feels safe, valued, and empowered to reach their full potential. We work with schools and communities to remove barriers to education and promote rights-based approaches that leave no child behind.

Children Rights Clubs

Establishing platforms in schools across Kenya where children can voice their concerns, learn about their rights, and advocate for themselves among peers and communities. These clubs strengthen child participation and create lasting awareness of child rights.
